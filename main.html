About Us

In 1948, my Great Grandfather Sherman Williams Jr. founded Apline Estates, and since then, we have sold over three thousand homes to customers across the Appalachians. What started as a small family-owned real estate business has grown into a trusted name in the industry, committed to providing exceptional service and expertise in every transaction. For over seven decades, we’ve been deeply rooted in the Appalachian community, helping families find their perfect homes, whether it’s a charming mountain retreat or a cozy cottage in a nearby town. Our team prides itself on upholding the values of integrity, trust, and a personalized approach, ensuring each client feels supported throughout their journey. At Apline Estates, we understand that buying or selling a home is not just a transaction—it’s a life-changing experience. That’s why we go the extra mile, providing guidance, insight, and resources every step of the way. Our long-standing legacy is a testament to our commitment to excellence and our passion for helping people find their place in the beautiful Appalachian landscape. Whether you’re a first-time homebuyer or a seasoned investor, we’re here to turn your real estate dreams into reality. Welcome to Apline Estates—where tradition meets trust.
